        About Privacy Policy Rules

        How to Play

        Basic Controls

        Space: generate a scramble and start inspection. Esc: abort the current solve. Keyboard controls follow a csTimer-style virtual cube layout.

        Solve Flow

        Press Space to scramble. A 15-second inspection begins. Your first valid move starts the timer. x, y, and z rotations do not start it. The timer stops when the cube is solved.

        Ranking Rules

        Only valid solves are submitted. Suspicious solves are excluded. Log in to join the online rankings.

        Battle Mode

        Friend Battle supports Virtual Cube and Real Cube rooms. The host starts each round with the players who are Ready. Ranked Battle matches another player automatically.
